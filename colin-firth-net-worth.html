Colin Firth: Career

Successful and accomplished actor Colin Firth is highly awarded for his acting skills.

Prominently, he is regarded as a part of the Brit Pack of young British actors, including Bruce Payne, Tim Roth, and Paul McGann.

His first work was in the wardrobe department at the National Theatre. Then, he went to study at the Drama Centre London and started appearing in stage productions.

The role of Mr. Darcy in the television adaptation of Jane Austen’s popular book, “Pride and Prejudice” made him visible in the industry in 1995.

Similarly, Colin appeared in theatre productions such as “Hamlet” and “Another Country” during the start of his career.
